How NotesCloud Works

Step 1: Log In

Login with the credentials provided by John. Once logged in, you can upload and download notes.

Step 2: Upload your notes.

Click the "Upload" button, select your file, and fill in the details to make your notes easy to find.

Step 3: Search for Notes

Use the search bar and filters to find the notes you need by subject, course, or title.

Step 4: Download Notes

Click the download button to get the notes in PDF, Word, or other formats.
